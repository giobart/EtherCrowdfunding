Save the Pandas

The panda foundation needs you

Due to an emergency many institutions and small entities, like organizations and groups of people, decide to open a crowdfunding campaign to contribute.
The panda foundation needs your help to save the life of little flaffy pandas even during this pandemic situation. In order to do so we use this Ethereum based campaign.
A crowdfunding based on a smart contract benefits from transparency and im-mutability.
The bytecode of the smart contracts can be easily visualized and, it is possible to prove its authenticity by comparing the compilation result with the one stored on the blockchain. Moreover, any user can check if the amount collected by the smart contract has been sent to the correct recipients. The smart contract code cannot be modified in a second moment, therefore granting to the campaign a safety measure.
